# GLOSSARY

- **XP**: Experience points earned from actions like Daily Check-ins. XP increases your Level.
- **Level**: Your progression rank, calculated from total XP.
- **Daily Check-in**: A once-per-day action that grants +10 XP and +1 to one selected stat.
- **Stats**: Your four growth attributes: Strength, Intellect, Wisdom, and Dexterity.
- **Path**: Your style identity (Warrior, Mage, Rogue, or Priest) chosen during onboarding.
- **Focus stat**: The one stat you prioritize at start to guide your progression intent.
- **Reduce motion**: Accessibility setting that limits animations based on system preference or app override.
- **Demo data**: A deterministic sample profile that preloads XP, stats, and history so the app is understandable immediately.
